About Me

My name is Muhammad Irfan Rafi, a student at Sriwijaya University, majoring in Computer Engineering. I have a deep interest in programming and technology development, and am committed to continuously improving my skills in designing, building, and developing effective and useful software. With a strong passion for learning, I aspire to become a professional programmer capable of providing innovative solutions through technology and contributing to the advancement of the IT industry.

My educational background has shaped my strong logical and analytical skills. My high school background at SMAN 1 Kawali, majoring in IPA, provided me with a foundation in science and mathematics, which I then developed in the D III Computer Engineering Study Program to deepen my knowledge of programming and technology.

I have participated in several useful activities and also received awards, such as participating in a computer competition exam in high school and also “River and Lake Safety Counseling for River and Lake Boat Operators.” These were held by government agencies.
